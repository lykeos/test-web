Project Information

In this project, I took on the role of a data analyst responsible for creating and delivering transaction reports through a dashboard for an e-commerce company. (Dataset from Kaggle).

Project Objectives

To assist stakeholders in monitoring and tracking real-time sales data from the company.

Tasks Performed

  1. Conducting EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis).
  2. Handling Missing and Duplicated Values
  3. Data Preprocessing by renaming columns and changing data types
  4. Performing Data Visualization using Python
  5. Creating Dashboards with Looker Studio

Conclusion

Conclusion

The total profit obtained from transaction processes between January 2015 and January 2016 amounted to $3,484,311.50. The product categories with high customer demand are fashion, home & furniture, contributing most of the profit from the Central, South, and EMEA regions, particularly from states like California, England, and New York.

Insights

  1. The total profit obtained is $3,484,311.50, with the highest profit recorded in December 2015.
  2. The highest profits are from the fashion and home & furniture categories.
  3. Total sales amount to $8,023,510.00, showing a stable sales graph throughout 2015.
  4. The company experienced a sales decline in early 2016, specifically between December 2015 and January 2016.
  5. The top three regions contributing to the highest profits are the Central, South, and EMEA regions.
  6. The top three states contributing to the highest profits are California, England, and New York.
  7. Electronic products have the lowest transaction volume in this company.

Recommendations

  1. Business Collaboration: Consider collaborating with external partners to expand product reach.
  2. Product Segmentation and Marketing: Focus on fashion and home & furniture categories for more targeted promotions.
  3. Optimal User Experience: Pay attention to website appearance, payment processes, and customer service to enhance transactions.
  4. Advanced Analysis with Analytical Tools: Utilize machine learning to predict purchasing trends and customer preferences.
  5. Offer Discounts and Promotions: Use discounts or loyalty programs to attract new customers.
