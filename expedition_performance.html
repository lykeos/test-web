Expedition Performance

Go Dashboard

Project Information

In this project, I played the role of a data analyst, assisting stakeholders in evaluating the performance of an expedition in Indonesia by providing insights and recommendations to facilitate decision-making regarding the issues at hand.

Project Objectives

To assist stakeholders in making decisions about issues by providing insights and recommendations from data analysis.

Tasks Performed

  1. Creating a datamart using SQL with MySQL DBMS.
  2. Checking data issues (missing values and duplicated values) using Python.
  3. Conducting Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) by performing univariate and multivariate analysis.
  4. Finding insights.
  5. Creating a dashboard and Data Storytelling using Tableau.

Conclusion

Conclusion

Our expedition's performance is still suboptimal due to frequent occurrences of late deliveries, influenced by significant discounts on products. Most of the products we send are lightweight but have high discount rates.

Insights

  1. Late deliveries tend to occur for products with high discounts.
  2. Significant discounts are given to lightweight products (below 4 kg), while products above 4 kg receive small discounts (0-19%).
  3. Products weighing below 3.5 kg tend to experience delayed deliveries.
  4. Lightweight products experiencing delays lead to increased customer calls to customer service.
  5. Product prices also influence the number of customer calls.

Recommendations

  1. Future shipments should not solely consider the discount aspect on ordered products to improve expedition performance and maintain customer trust.
  2. If possible, revise the discount policy on products, considering more moderate discounts or different shipping rules.
